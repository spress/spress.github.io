Spress

The static site generator in PHP

Download 2.2.0

by Zach Frailey

Spress is a static site generator which allows you to write and publish your web sites

Spress allows you to create and deploy blogs, personal websites, simple corporate websites with information about your products and even landing pages for your services.

Customize it completely

You can write themes and plugins to make it more awesome.

Powerful templates

Create amazing templates powered by Twig template engine.

Simple writing

You can create your blog or web site with your favorite text editor in Markdown.

No backend

Spress is a CMS powered by flat files, meaning that no database is required. You only need a simple web server!.

Spress is open source

Spress is completely free and open source, released under the MIT license.
